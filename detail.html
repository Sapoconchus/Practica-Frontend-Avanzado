A light, crisp and bitter IPA brewed with English and American hops. A small batch brewed only once.

Firstly brewed on: 09/2007

Brewer tip: The earthy and floral aromas from the hops can be overpowering. Drop a little Cascade in at the end of the boil to lift the profile with a bit of citrus.

Ingredients: malt (Maris Otter Extra Pale, Caramalt, Munich), hops (Fuggles, First Gold, Cascade)
